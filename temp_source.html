About Us

Bringing truth back to Christian media.

Who We Are

Mehare Christian Television Channel exists to share the pure and accurate Word of God in the Amharic language. We focus on teaching the Bible in a way that is clear, honest, and centered on Christ alone.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of believers who understand Scripture, grow in spiritual maturity, and are able to recognize and resist false teachings.

Our Mission

  • Teaching the Bible in simple and clear language
  • Creating worship and music that honor God
  • Hosting real conversations about life and faith
  • Sharing stories of what God is doing

Our Values

Christ First Truth Humility Clarity Integrity Peace

Our Programs

Content designed to edify, educate, and encourage.

Bible Teaching

This is the heart of Mehare TV. Our Bible teaching programs focus on clear, simple, and accurate explanation of Scripture. Each teaching is designed to help viewers understand the true meaning of the Bible without confusion or exaggeration.

We explain passages verse by verse, discuss real-life application, and make sure everything stays centered on Christ. These teachings are short, practical, and easy for anyone to follow.

Worship & Music

Our worship and music programs are created to bring viewers into a peaceful and Christ-centered atmosphere. We produce original Christian songs that are biblically sound and spiritually uplifting.

Every song is written with care, focusing on truth rather than performance. We aim to create worship that helps believers reflect on God's goodness.

Podcasts

The podcast programs offer honest conversations about the daily challenges people face. We talk about identity, relationships, struggles, questions of faith, and real issues.

Each episode is friendly, relatable, and grounded in the Bible. The goal is to create a space where people feel understood and encouraged.

Reports & Documentaries

These programs share stories of what God is doing in Ethiopia and beyond. We cover church events, community activities, testimonies, and movements that bring spiritual impact. Our reports are designed to inform, inspire, and encourage believers by showing how God is working in different places.

Short Films

Our short films use creative storytelling to communicate spiritual truths in a simple and relatable way. Each film is based on a biblical principle and shows how that truth can be lived in daily life. The goal is to help viewers see God's truth in action through characters and situations that feel real.
